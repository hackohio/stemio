About the Event

STEM I/O is a one day hands-on exploration of STEM hosted by OHI/O and CoolTechGirls. We aim to provide students in grades 5 -8 an opportunity to learn about science, technology, engineering, and math in a fun, engaging, and inclusive environment. This event takes place on November 16, from 10 am – 2 pm. Lunch and snacks will be provided.

Join us for a day filled with discovery and innovation as students move from table to table, exploring a variety of fascinating topics in STEM. From animation to velocity, and everything in between,  each interactive station guides students through experiments and demonstrations. Students will get to bring their projects home with them. Don't miss this opportunity to ignite a passion for STEM in the next generation!
